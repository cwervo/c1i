Fall 2024

Location: Parsons 2 W 13th, Room 1111
Instructor: Andrés Cuervo

Core 1: Interaction is designed to introduce students to programming as a creative medium, way of making, a mode of exploring design. The coursework focuses on developing a vocabulary of interaction design principles which can then be applied across a range of platforms. Students are encouraged to experiment with various media, tools, and techniques, ultimately producing a portfolio of interactive and visual projects designed for the screen. An emphasis is placed on typography as it applies to a screen context, research-based problem solving and a learning-through-making approach to technical skill building. Historical and current interaction design precedents will be discussed.
